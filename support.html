Support Policy

Wisdom is committed to providing a respectful, accurate, and spiritually grounded experience. The following outlines how we handle support requests.

What we can help with

  • Technical issues — crashes, login problems, notification or widget failures
  • Subscription and billing questions — charges, upgrades, and cancellations
  • Content concerns — inaccuracies in Sanskrit text, translations, or explanations
  • Account and data requests — deleting your account or exported journal entries
  • General feedback and feature suggestions

Response time

We aim to respond to all support emails within 1–2 business days. More complex content reviews may take up to 5 business days.

Content accuracy

The shlokas and explanations in Wisdom are curated with care from traditional commentaries and scholarly sources. If you believe any content is inaccurate, mistranslated, or presented in a culturally insensitive way, please email us with the specific shloka reference and your concern. We take content feedback seriously and will review every report.

Subscriptions & refunds

Wisdom offers a free tier and an optional Pro subscription managed through the App Store. Subscription billing, cancellation, and refund requests are handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. If you have trouble with this process, email us and we'll guide you through it.

Privacy

Journal entries you write in the Wisdom app are stored privately on your device and are never shared with us or any third party. We do not sell your data. For any privacy-related questions, email us at work.ankurshukla@gmail.com.

You can also reach us at work.ankurshukla@gmail.com — we read every email.
